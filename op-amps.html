Operational Amplifiers

Difference engines, sculpting signal and silence.

Op-amps compare voltages and amplify the difference — a kind of analog thinking. With near-infinite gain and elegant feedback, they form the backbone of filters, mixers, and control systems.

  • ⚖️ Differential input and high gain
  • 🔁 Negative feedback and stability
  • 🎚 Mixers, integrators, and comparators
  • 🎛 Filter design and envelope shaping

In modular synthesis, op-amps are everywhere — hidden inside filters, VCAs, and utility modules. They don’t just amplify; they decide, blend, and shape.
