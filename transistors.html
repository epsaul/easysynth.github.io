Transistors

Switches that think, amplifiers that whisper.

Transistors are the hinge between silence and signal — switching, amplifying, shaping logic and tone. They form the foundation of modern electronics and modular control.

  • 🔁 NPN, PNP, MOSFET, BJT
  • 🔊 Amplification and gain control
  • 🧠 Logic gates and sequencer triggers
  • 🎚 VCAs, envelope followers, and signal routing

In synthesis, transistors are the silent operators behind voltage-controlled amplifiers and envelope shaping — the invisible hands that sculpt sound.
