Inductors

Coiled memory, resisting change.

Inductors store energy in magnetic fields, opposing changes in current like a stubborn echo. Their resistance to change gives rise to time delays, filtering, and resonance.

  • 🌀 Magnetic flux and inductance
  • ⏱ RL circuits and exponential decay
  • 🎛 Passive filters and analog tone shaping
  • 🧵 Coils, toroids, and chokes

Though less common in synth modules than capacitors, inductors whisper through vintage filters and power rails, shaping the flow with quiet persistence.
